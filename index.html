Your Luxury Crete Apartment Awaits

Nema Made is a modern 2-bedroom luxury apartment in Made, Heraklion, ideal for up to 4 guests. Located just steps from Lygaria Beach and Made Beach, it offers stunning sea views and an excellent base for exploring Crete’s coastline and nearby attractions.

The apartment features a fully equipped kitchen, stylish living area, two comfortable bedrooms, and a modern bathroom within 59 sqm of well-designed space. A private heated outdoor jacuzzi with sea views provides the perfect setting to relax and unwind.

Set near Agia Pelagia, Nema Made combines easy access to restaurants, beaches, and entertainment with the privacy of a peaceful retreat. Perfect for couples, families, or friends, this luxury stay delivers authentic Cretan hospitality—book early to secure your preferred dates.
